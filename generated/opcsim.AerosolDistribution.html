opcsim.AerosolDistribution¶

class opcsim.AerosolDistribution(label=None)[source]¶

Define an aerosol distribution.

We are operating on the assumption that any aerosol distribution can be described as the sum of n lognormal modes following Seinfeld and Pandis equation 8.54.

\[n_N^o(logD_p)=Σ_{i=1}^{n}\frac{N_i}{\sqrt{2π} logσ_i}exp(-\frac{(logD_p - logD̄_p)^2}{2log^2σ_i})\]
